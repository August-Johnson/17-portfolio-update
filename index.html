About Me

My name is August, but I mostly go by Gus. In my spare time, I mainly play video games and listen to music. I enjoy learning about history and law and keeping up with politics. My favorite animals are cats. I have a dog named Daisy and a cat named Magic. I graduated from high school in 2016 and worked at Lunds & Byerlys for about a year. Once I found out about coding and by extension this boot camp, it seemed like a good fit. Before I started my class, I shadowed the front end accessibility development team at U.S. Bank for an entire day. During that day I met some amazing people who taught me a lot about the important core fundamentals and how to ensure your code is accessible. I absorbed so much that day, and it fueled my desire to learn and succeed even more.
